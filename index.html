Hardaware Identification Chart

Model Name Description
Motherboard The Motherboard holds together many of the crucial parts of the computer.
Hard Drive The Hard Drive stores permanent data onto the computer.
PCIe Card (Graphics Card) The Graphics Card renders things quicker and makes the quality of things on the screen better.
Power Supply The Power Supply provides power to the computer.
DVD Drive The DVD Drive reads data off of a DVD, and can also store data on one too.
